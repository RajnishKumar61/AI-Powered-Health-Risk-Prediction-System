Heart disease refers to conditions affecting the heart and blood vessels, including coronary artery disease
and heart failure.
Major risk factors include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, diabetes, smoking, obesity, and physical inactivity.
Symptoms may include chest pain, shortness of breath, dizziness, and fatigue.
Elevated cholesterol ratios and persistent hypertension significantly increase cardiovascular risk.
Heart disease is one of the leading causes of mortality worldwide.
Preventive measures include blood pressure control, cholesterol management, healthy diet, and regular exercise.
