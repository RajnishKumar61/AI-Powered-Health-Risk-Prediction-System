Liver disease includes conditions such as fatty liver disease, hepatitis, and cirrhosis.
Risk factors include excessive alcohol consumption, obesity, diabetes, viral infections, and certain medications.
Liver disease can impair metabolism, detoxification, and protein synthesis.
Symptoms may include fatigue, abdominal discomfort, loss of appetite, and jaundice in advanced cases.
Fatty liver disease is commonly associated with metabolic syndrome and insulin resistance.
Lifestyle changes, including weight management and reduced alcohol intake, are key preventive strategies.
